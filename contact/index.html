Arrival By Plane

Vienna’s public transport provides frequent connections between the airport and the city via bus or railway. To see the details of your 1h trip from the airport to our building, enter Flughafen Wien and Erzherzog Johann Platz 1 in this form or that form.

Get an airport ticket.

If you are in a hurry, you might prefer the City Airport Train (CAT) over busses to avoid potential delays due to traffic jams.

Furthermore shuttle services and taxis are available.

From Vienna main station

Take the underground U1 towards Leopoldau and exit at the next station, Taubstummengasse. Alternatively, you can take a walk downward on Favoritenstraße.
